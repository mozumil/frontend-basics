Here's a timeline of Iron Man's journey:

  • 2012 - Becomes founding member of The Avengers
  • 2015 - Creates Ultron in an effort to achieve peace, which backfires
  • 2016 - Leads one side of the Avengers in Civil War
  • 2018 - Battles Thanos in Infinity War
  • 2019 - Sacrifices himself in Endgame to save the universe
